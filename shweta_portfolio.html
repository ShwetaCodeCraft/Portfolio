The Story
From a girl who dreamed of flying cockpits to one who builds full stack systems — the drive never changed.

I'm Shweta — a final-year Computer Science student at RVS College of Engineering and Technology, Jamshedpur. I started engineering with a love for aviation and defence (yes, that flight simulator was from my first year), and somehow ended up falling even harder for software.
